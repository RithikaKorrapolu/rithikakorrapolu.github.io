Commit to the Bit Card Game

Concept

A party card game where players try to execute various bits without getting caught. There are three different levels based on difficulty of the bit. Complete all three levels throughout the course of the party you and win. Think "Modern day Charades meets We're Not Really Strangers"

I'm collecting bits from people - If you have one, please insert here!!

Mock-Ups

Background

This started as a light weight experiment for an apartment party I hosted where I invited different groups of friends over and wanted them to skip the small talk and be playful with each other. So I put up different sticky notes on my door and everyone had to pick one and read it before entering. Each sticky note had a different bit - ranging from a silly quote someone had to say to a prank they needed to complete on someone. Throughout the party, I saw my friends come up with back stories and distractions and interesting ways to complete their bit in an organic and unsuspecting way. At the end of the evening, we revealed the different bits and who fell for what.

I thought it could be fun to play again so I thought about turning it into a card game. If you have any good bits, please feel free to submit them here!!

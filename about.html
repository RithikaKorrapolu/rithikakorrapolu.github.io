About Me

“I’m so embarrassed. I’m not a real person yet.”
- from Frances Ha

Born in 1997, I was raised in Paradise by my earnest and hard working family. I live in NYC now but still visit them often.

“When you tell [adults] about a new friend, they never ask questions about what really matters. They never ask:
‘What does his voice sound like?’
‘What games does he like best?’
‘Does he collect butterflies?’
They ask:
‘How old is he?’
‘How many brothers does he have?’
‘How much does he weigh?’
‘How much money does he have?’
Only then do they think they know him.”
- from The Little Prince

My favorite color is this orange. My favorite animal is a panda. I know how to do three magic tricks (but mess up the third one a lot so it’s more like two). I get the most frustrated when I can’t understand someone I care about. I’m allergic to tree nuts. I like doing bad improv. I really like getting my friends to do bad improv. I know that I’m a sensitive person but I don’t like it when people call me that. My favorite compliment I've ever gotten is that I "have a disarming personality" and I'm pretty sure I can do the macarena to any song.

And for the Adults: I graduated with degrees in Computer Science and Business. I work on the Responsible AI team at Microsoft - I work with research and engineering people. My job is cool but I probably get equally if not more excited to work on all my side endeavors

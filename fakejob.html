About the team

We're a small team based in NYC. We work on random-ass-stuff™ like this. We have world-class "partners, clients, and investors" (friends) who work across healthcare, technology, finance, art, you name it. They've raised money and made news and won awards. But more importantly, they always try to do a lot more good than bad. They are forgiving and laugh easily. They are all-around superstar brilliant and they make our team worth being on!

About the role

The people you work with are usually the people you spend the most time in the day with. The ones you give the most energy to. You're probably giving them more "life force" than your own family and friends. That's crazy, no? Shouldn't we take hiring way more seriously? I mean companies do take it seriously but not THAT seriously. It feels like the main intent behind most job applications (with the questions about education and prior work) is "Can you do work that will ultimately make my business more money? How much more?" Sure, that's important. But if you're potentially spending everyday with this person, sacrificing Life with your family for them, aren't there much more important questions? Like do they make you laugh? If you fck up, will you feel safe around them? Will they actually be a good friend?

In this role, you'll:

  • Spend a lot of time with the team (like a lot, like more than your friends and family)
  • Fail with the team
  • succeed with the team
  • do everything, with the team

Basic Qualifications

  • NYC based so we can hang

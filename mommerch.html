Only For You (Merch)

Personalized Merch Based on My Mom

I liked the idea of making a whole merch collection based on phrases or things that my mom likes and getting her to model them. I think this is generally a cool gift or thing to do for anyone you love. I want to do it more. This collection includes:

  • a bowl that says "We have food at home"
  • a mug that says "so when are you getting married"
  • a bag that says "Got any discount?"
  • a hat that says "my friend's son is a doctor"
